# Vietnam EV Market Context

> Status: APPROVED | Last Updated: 2026-03-01 | Source: Kickoff Document

---

## 1. Market Overview

Vietnam's EV market is experiencing rapid growth driven by government policies promoting green transportation, increasing environmental awareness, and domestic manufacturers like VinFast. The EV charging infrastructure is in early stages, creating significant opportunity for independent networks.

K-Charge aims to establish an **open charging ecosystem** — allowing multiple EV brands to access shared infrastructure rather than being locked into single-manufacturer ecosystems.

---

## 2. Strategic Vision

The client plans to establish a subsidiary company in Vietnam to operate an independent EV charging station network. Strategic goals include building an open ecosystem for all EV brands, maximizing station utilization through monitoring and optimized pricing, enabling data-driven decisions via dashboards and analytics, and creating a scalable digital foundation for a sustainable charging network.

---

## 3. Competitive Landscape

The Vietnam market features manufacturer-tied networks (e.g., VinFast's proprietary network) and a growing but fragmented independent charging segment. K-Charge differentiates through brand-agnostic approach, OCPP standard compliance for hardware vendor flexibility, and integrated digital experience via mobile app and admin portal.

---

## 4. Regulatory Environment

Key regulatory considerations for Vietnam include data protection laws requiring encrypted personal data, financial transaction log retention for audit, tax calculation compliance with local regulations, e-invoicing requirements (MISA, Viettel, VNPT), and PCI-DSS compliance for payment processing.

---

## 5. Payment Landscape

The system integrates with Vietnam's most popular digital payment platforms:

- **ZaloPay** — Leading mobile payment by VNG Corporation
- **MoMo** — Vietnam's largest e-wallet
- **OnePay** — Multi-channel payment gateway

All payments are cashless. System supports prepay, domestic payment gateways, and e-wallets.

---

## 6. Design Preferences (Client UI Reference)

The client specified mobile app UI/UX preferences: color tone of blue, white, and orange; smart navigation by vehicle type; news updates and promotional programs; invoice checking in profile section; PCI-DSS certified payment gateway display; and clean, modern design emphasizing the station map and real-time availability.
