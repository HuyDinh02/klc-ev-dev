# Stakeholder Analysis

> Status: APPROVED | Last Updated: 2026-03-01 | Source: BRD v0.1

---

## 1. Internal Key Stakeholders

### Executive Sponsor
- **Role:** Approves investment and budget, defines strategic direction, oversees business expansion
- **Interest:** Market positioning in EV ecosystem, ROI, competitive differentiation
- **Influence Level:** Very High

### Business Owner / Product Owner
- **Role:** Defines business requirements, prioritizes features, approves deliverables
- **Interest:** Operational efficiency, revenue optimization, customer satisfaction
- **Influence Level:** High

### Operations Team
- **Role:** Manages charging stations daily, monitors charger status, coordinates field operations
- **Interest:** Real-time monitoring, minimal downtime, easy station configuration
- **Influence Level:** Medium-High

### Technical Support / Maintenance Team
- **Role:** Handles charger faults, responds to alerts, manages maintenance tickets
- **Interest:** Accurate fault diagnostics, clear escalation workflow, maintenance tracking
- **Influence Level:** Medium

### Finance & Accounting Team
- **Role:** Revenue monitoring, payment reconciliation, tax reporting compliance
- **Interest:** Accurate billing calculation, transparent transaction logs, Vietnamese regulation compliance
- **Influence Level:** Medium

### IT / System Administration Team
- **Role:** Infrastructure management, security configuration, system performance monitoring
- **Interest:** System stability, cybersecurity, scalability
- **Influence Level:** Medium

---

## 2. External Key Stakeholders

### EV Drivers (End Users)
- **Role:** Use mobile app to find stations, initiate and pay for charging sessions
- **Interest:** Easy-to-use experience, transparent pricing, reliable availability
- **Influence Level:** Medium (High impact on brand reputation)

### Charging Hardware Suppliers
- **Role:** Provide physical stations, support OCPP integration, deliver firmware updates
- **Interest:** Stable integration, protocol compatibility, performance monitoring
- **Influence Level:** Medium

### Payment Gateway Providers (ZaloPay, MoMo, OnePay)
- **Role:** Process digital transactions, ensure payment security
- **Interest:** Secure integration, transaction volume, regulatory compliance
- **Influence Level:** Medium

### Government & Regulatory Authorities
- **Role:** Define regulatory framework, monitor data protection, oversee tax compliance
- **Interest:** Legal compliance, consumer protection, energy market regulation
- **Influence Level:** Medium
