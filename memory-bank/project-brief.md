# Project Brief — EV Charging CSMS

## What
EV Charging Station Management System for KLC (Vietnam). B2C platform managing charging stations with Admin Portal + Mobile App.

## Who
- **Client:** KLC (establishing subsidiary in Vietnam)
- **Developer:** EmeSoft (CTO: Hung, 5 developers)
- **Users:** EV drivers (mobile app), operators/admin (web portal)

## Why
Build an open EV charging ecosystem in Vietnam. Brand-agnostic, not locked to single manufacturer. Scalable digital foundation.

## Timeline
- Kick-off: March 1, 2026
- Prototype: March 15, 2026
- MVP: June 1, 2026 (4 months total)

## Scope
### Phase 1 (MVP)
- Station & connector management, real-time monitoring, energy metering, fault detection
- Tariff configuration, payment (ZaloPay/MoMo/OnePay), e-invoicing
- Mobile: vehicle management, QR charging, real-time status, payment, history

### Phase 2
- Idle fee, ops manual override, firmware lifecycle
- AI: energy optimization, anomaly detection, predictive maintenance
